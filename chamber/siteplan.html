Site Name

São Paulo Chamber of Commerce

Site Purpose

This website will serve as a resource hub for businesses in the city of São Paulo. The site will provide information on local events, host networking opportunities, create business directories, and encourage local shopping. It aims to promote local economic growth, support local businesses, and foster a sense of community between the businesses.e

Target Market

Business owners and patrons in the city of São Paulo and surrounding areas.

Site Goals

Improve member engagement by providing valuable resources and networking opportunities.

Attract new businesses and encourage economic development in the community.

Improve the visibility and reputation of the chamber as a trusted authority in local business matters.

Small Business Owner:

Ricardo is a bishop who runs his business as a virtual accounting office. He is looking for networking opportunities, business resources and marketing support to help grow his business.

Business Owner:

Matheus is a full systems analyst looking for ways to grow his business. His consultancy is interested in partnership opportunities, mainly with programmers, for the implementation and support of the Totvs-Protheus system.

The New Pastry Chef

Conceição recently opened a pastry shop after her retirement. She is looking for partnerships to help publicize her business and new clients to help grow her business.

Scenarios

A local business owner is interested in joining the chamber of commerce to network with other business owners. They visit the website to find information on membership benefits, fees, and how to apply.

A community member is looking for upcoming events and workshops hosted by the chamber. They visit the website to browse the events calendar and register for interesting activities.

A new baker who is starting out in her new field and wants to know more about the local business environment. Explore the chamber's website for information on existing businesses, the availability of marketing and publicity help and profiles of people in the community.

SEO Plan

Use relevant keywords in site descriptions, content, and blog posts.

Verify the site on Google's Business Profile.

Get inbound links or backlinks from all member business to improve rankings.

Embed Google Analytics in all site pages.
