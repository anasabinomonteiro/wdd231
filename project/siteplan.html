Site Name

Audience

Young Women leaders

Site Purpose

The site will be a platform dedicated to supporting Young Women leaders (young women ages 12 to 18) in Brazil, offering activity ideas, helpful resources, and guidance based on the General Church Handbook. The goal is to create a centralized and accessible space where leaders can find inspiration, exchange experiences and strengthen their work with Young Women, as well as facilitate communication and planning of activities relevant to the spiritual and social development of Young Women.

Scenarios

What spiritual and recreational activities can I plan for Young Women that are engaging and aligned with the General Handbook?

Our website content will offer tried-and-tested activity ideas, planning guidelines, and additional resources.

How can I help Young Women understand and practice Youth Strength guidelines through activities?

Many leaders look for practical ways to guide Young Women in applying the program. Our website offers suggestions for activities related to the program.
